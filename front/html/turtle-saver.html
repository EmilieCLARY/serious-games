Point info

Sooner or later, the plastic that pollutes our environment ends up in the oceans and threatens marine wildlife. One species in particular is the symbol of the ravages of plastic waste on the ocean: Sea turtles. For turtles, "biofouling" plastic waste, i.e. covered in algae and other organisms floating in the ocean, gives off the same smell as their food. They can easily mistake a piece of plastic covered with all kinds of organisms for a jellyfish or a fish. In short, it is very important not to throw away your plastic waste just anywhere. This will preserve the environment of the sea turtles but also of many other species.
